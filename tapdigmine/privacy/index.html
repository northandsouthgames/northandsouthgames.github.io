Privacy Policy

Tap Dig Mine · North & South Games · Effective July 5, 2026

North & South Games ("we," "us," or "our") operates the mobile game Tap Dig Mine (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you play the Game, we may collect the following:

  • Game progress and account data. Save data such as depth reached, currency, upgrades, achievements, settings, and a player display name used on leaderboards. Progress is stored on your device and may be synced to our cloud backend (Supabase) so you can recover your save.
  • Anonymous account identifiers. When you play, we create or use an anonymous account ID to associate your save and leaderboard entries with your device.
  • Google Sign-In (optional). If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive information from Google such as your name, email address, and profile picture, linked to your game account for cloud save and account recovery.
  • Leaderboard data. Your display name and score may be shown on public leaderboards.
  • Purchase information. In-app purchases (such as gem packs or "Remove Ads") are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We receive confirmation of purchases to grant in-game entitlements; payment details are handled by the store, not by us.
  • Advertising data. The Game shows optional rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may collect device identifiers, ad interaction data, and similar information to serve and measure ads. In the European Economic Area and other regions, we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain consent where required.
  • Analytics data. We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the Game is used (for example, session activity and feature usage). This helps us improve stability and gameplay.
  • Technical data. Device type, operating system version, app version, and crash or performance logs may be collected automatically by our services and third-party SDKs.

2. How We Use Information

  • Save, restore, and sync your game progress
  • Operate leaderboards and display player names and scores
  • Authenticate optional Google-linked accounts
  • Deliver rewarded ads and honor ad-removal purchases
  • Process and validate in-app purchases
  • Analyze usage to fix bugs and improve the Game
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, we process personal data on these bases:

  • Contract: to provide the Game, cloud saves, and purchases you request
  • Legitimate interests: to secure the Game, prevent abuse, and improve our services
  • Consent: for personalized advertising where required (managed through UMP and your device settings)

4. Third-Party Services

The Game uses trusted third parties that process data under their own policies:

  • Supabase — authentication and cloud save storage
  • Google — Google Sign-In, Firebase Analytics, AdMob, and UMP consent
  • Google Play / Apple App Store — in-app purchase processing

5. Data Retention

We keep game save and account data while your account is active or as needed to provide the Game. Leaderboard entries may remain visible after account deletion unless removed by us. Analytics and ad-related data are retained according to each provider's policies. You may delete local data by uninstalling the Game.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • Advertising: You can limit ad personalization in your device settings. EU/EEA users may manage consent through the in-app consent form.
  • Account linking: Google Sign-In is optional. You may continue as an anonymous guest.
  • Access and deletion: To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, contact us at the email below. We will respond within a reasonable time and as required by applicable law.
  • California residents: You may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know, delete, and opt out of certain sharing. We do not sell personal information as defined by California law.

7. Children

Tap Dig Mine is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our service providers operate. We rely on appropriate safeguards where required by law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page and update the effective date. Continued use of the Game after changes means you accept the updated policy.
